Prompt: Generate a single p5.js sketch file that draws a fractal tree on a black 400x400 canvas.
Add sliders to control the length of branches, and the angle of the branches.
Place the sliders at the bottom of the canvas with the label and value of the sliders to the left and the sliders to the right.
Have the sliders span the width of the canvas less the width of the label and value.
Make each step of the tree have a color of the rainbow (red, orange, yellow, green, blue, violet).
Have the root of the tree have a stroke width of 7 and get smaller as you go up the tree.

Challenge: Add two additional sliders that control the depth of the recursion and number of branches.
